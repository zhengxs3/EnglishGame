rules of the game
Equipment :
2 packs of Persona cards with different colors to distinguish the difference between player 1 and 2 (16 cards per pack): Each card represents a persona, with specific characteristics such as age, interest, buying behavior, values, skills and devices.
Number of participants :
2 players confronting each other
Objective of the Game
The aim of the game is to guess which persona your opponent has chosen by asking closed-ended questions (“yes” or “no” answers). You must be the first to discover the other player's persona.
How to Play
These cards are placed face-up on the table.
A paper is distributed at the beginning where you can find on it all of the personas, to check the one you pick. Each player secretly chooses a persona from the 16 available in their deck.
Players take turns asking closed-ended questions (“yes” or “no” answers) to guess which persona their opponent has chosen.
Player A begins by asking a closed question about his opponent's characteristics. Example question: Does your persona buy on the Internet?
Player B answers “yes” or “no”. Depending on the answer, Player A must eliminate certain cards from his own deck, to get as close as possible to the persona chosen by his opponent.
If the answer is “no” to the question “Does your persona buy on the Internet?”, player A must eliminate all cards whose persona does not buy on the Internet.
It's the other player's turn to ask a question. The players continue in this way, each taking a turn to ask a question until one of them finds the right persona.
The first player to guess the opponent's persona wins the game. The player must say: I think your persona is ...
The game ends as soon as one player correctly guesses the opponent's persona. If a player has eliminated all his cards without guessing the other player's persona, he loses the game.
Cards Rules
Be Careful, specific cards exist ! Each time a player has to ask a question, they have to pick a card among the constraint cards to add a touch of difficulty.
Prohibition
do not ask physical or age-related questions
